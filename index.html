About Us

.NET Book Study was a happy accident. It started off as a couple of strangers from different sides of the globe looking to meet new people, as well as sharpen their .NET skills. It has now propelled into friends creating YouTube and Twitch content, and building a .NET community for all!

Not only is .NET Book Study a safe space to learn how to code, it is also a space where we can explore the impact of our code. Whether you are a Code Newbie, an expert, or somewhere in between, .NET Book Study is a space where we are actively standing up to Imposter Syndrome together. We are believing in ourselves, our abilities, and our vision! This is a space where it is okay to ask questions. It is okay to not know everything. It is okay to try a new approach.

.NET Book Study is about creating a New Story.
